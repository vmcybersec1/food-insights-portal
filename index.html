Pick. Reveal. Swap.

Use this like a food detective game at the school table. Visitors choose what they eat and learn one simple improvement.

1. Ask

“What did you eat today?” Choose breakfast, lunchbox, snack, drink, dinner or treat.

2. Reveal

Show concern score, processing level, sugar, salt, fat and additives.

3. Swap

End with a positive better alternative instead of just saying “bad food”.

Built as a lightweight static demo. No forms, logins, tracking or database.
