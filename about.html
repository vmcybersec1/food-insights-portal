About this demo

A simple food detective website for a school project.

This static website helps children and parents talk about everyday food choices. It is designed for a short public demo and does not collect any personal data.

What the scores mean

The concern score is a simplified educational guide based on processing level, sugar, salt, fat, additives, packaging and how suitable the item is as an everyday choice.

  • 0–4: lower concern or everyday choice.
  • 5–7: okay sometimes, check the label and portion.
  • 8–10: best kept as an occasional treat.

Important note

This is not medical or dietetic advice. It is a school-friendly learning tool to encourage curiosity, label checking and better swaps.
